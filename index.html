📜 Battle Scrolls

🎯 Mission

Claim the most glory across all rounds by keeping your sworn totems near the PEAK of the ancient stack when the scoring ritual ends each round.

𓆩😈𓆪 The Ancient Stack

9 totem idols rise in a random tower each round. Position #1 is the summit — the seat of supreme power. Each warrior secretly fights for different idols to rule the peak.

🔐 Sworn Totems

Each warrior privately receives 3 sworn totem targets — visible only to YOU via the "👁 Reveal" button. Conceal them before passing to the next warrior!

Secrecy: During setup each warrior views their oath in private. In battle, tap "👁 Reveal" to see your targets, then tap "🙈 Conceal" before others can see.

⚔️ Battle Cards — One Per Turn

Each warrior wields cards each round. Play ONE card per turn — command passes automatically after you strike.

⬆️
Rise 1/2/3
Propel any totem UP 1, 2, or 3 positions in the tower
💥
Topple
Hurl any totem all the way to the base of the tower
🔥
Banish
Destroy the base totem. Cannot use on your very first strike.

⏳ Round Ends When

  • 6 totems have been banished (only 3 remain), OR
  • Every warrior has exhausted all their battle cards

Wage war for 4 rounds (2 warriors), 3 rounds (3 warriors), or 4 rounds (4 warriors). Most glory wins. A tie triggers a sudden-death tiebreaker!
